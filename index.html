DatAndroid

DatAndroid was built by the semi-automatic exploration of android apps downloaded from Uptodown emulated on 14 different devices with the screenshots and associated metadata(View Hierarchies). The dataset contains data from about 95 unique apps of the Writing and Notes category, containing mostly utilities to manage and organize text-based content and item lists. The exposes data are used to evaluate different image recognition algorithms' usability to support the visual Gui testing on different devices. We apply two different matching algorithms (Fullscreen and Cropped) to identify widgets' visual locators making repeatable tests.

Feature Matching-based Approaches to Improve the Robustness of Android Visual GUI Testing

Android automated Visual GUI Testing (VGT) tools have low robustness to device fragmentation, i.e., scripts rarely lead to correct results when running on different devices than the one where they were captured. We evaluate two feature matching-based approaches for widget detection in VGT scripts, which respectively use full-screen snapshots (Fullscreen) or cropped images of individual widgets (Cropped) as visual locators to match on emulated devices. We validated the portability of locators over different apps and devices, and we applied our approaches to a real test suite to evaluate their robustness in terms of cross-device portability and correctly executed interactions. The performance was compared against two state-of-the-art tools, EyeAutomate and Sikuli. Our Fullscreen approach outperformed state-of-the-art tools in terms of correctly identified locators across a wide range of devices, and led to a 30% increase in passing tests. A small price is paid in terms of execution time. The dependability of VGT tools can be enhanced by bridging the testing and computer vision communities, to design more robust image processing algorithms targeted to the specific needs of the domain. The results and dataset we collected can provide guidance for making mobile VGT more usable, and robust. PDF

Collection procedure

DatAndroid was build emulating Android apps via a casual exploration of the app environment. The process involves downloading apk files from the upToDown site installed on emulators without access or modification to source code. With Appium server commands, we explore application retrieving screenshot and View Hierarchies of the same activity emulated on every different android device. A visual check phase verifies the screenshots recovered to avoid sudden crashes or popups.
