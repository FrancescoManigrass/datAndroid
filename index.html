DatAndroid

DatAndroid was built by the semi-automatic exploration of android apps downloaded from Uptodown emulated on 14 different devices with the screenshots and associated metadata(View Hierarchies) retrieved directly from emulators. The dataset contains data from about 95 unique apps of Writing and Notes category, containing mostly utilities to manage and organize text-based content and item lists. The exposes data are used to evaluate the usability of different image recognition algorithms to support the visual Gui testing on different devices. In this work, we apply two different matching algorithms (Fullscreen and Cropped) to identify widgets’ visual locators making a repeatable a series of tests.
